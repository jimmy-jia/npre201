Peltier Tiles - Potential Applications

When we started to think of potential application we thought of places we could apply the peltier tiles to. We thought of applying them to places that lose heat and would try to harness energy that just escapes. In the real world there are plenty of applications used, including a flashlight developed to use the heat from your hands to power small LED lights.
