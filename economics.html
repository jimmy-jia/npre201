Points of note:

  • Cost of electricity in Illinois : $0.075
  • Cost of peltier tile: $23.99 for 10 ($2.399 each)
  • At low differences in temperature we found it to not be economically efficient
  • Around the 20°C temperature difference it becomes more economically efficient.

We can see from our data that these tiles would be effective in places with temperature differences of 20 degrees celsius. This does limit the number of places where it would be effective to utilize the tiles, but in those places, it would be an efficient source of power.

The main benefits of using the tiles would be the near linear scalability, and relatively low costs for the average family to invest in this form of power. At the very least it should be sufficient to power lights for a family on days where the temperature difference is sufficient.
